  • Molecular and Biochemistry: pulse chase radio-labeling, qRT-PCR, RNA/DNA extraction, plasmid construction, BAC2H, CRISPR-FINDeM.
  • Amphibian Ecology & Infectious Disease: metabolite challenge assays, field ecology, animal husbandry, sterile isolation of pathogens, environmental sampling.
  • Computational Biology/CS: Bioinformatical analysis on WGS and variant calling DNA, microsatellite analysis, R & Python Programming, website construction.
